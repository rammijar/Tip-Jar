Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2025

This app, PiTip, is a simple tipping tool for Pi Network users. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

  • Your Pi Network username (only when you log in)
  • Basic payment info (tip amount, payment status)

How We Use It

We use this information only to process your tip and thank you. We do not store or sell your data.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

Security

All transactions are handled securely through the official Pi Network SDK.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us via Pi Chat or through our GitHub page.
